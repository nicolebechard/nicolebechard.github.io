About

👥 LinkedIn ✉ nicole.bechard@gmail.com

I've spent over a decade at the intersection of data and product, building tools that help business users move from messy, disparate data to real decisions. That thread runs through everything I've worked on, from predictive analytics for nonprofit fundraisers to AI-powered insights for CRM platforms used by thousands of organizations worldwide.

I've always told my teams they can change my mind, and I mean it. Some of my best product decisions came from someone pushing back on my first instinct. I've invested seriously in understanding what makes teams work at their best. I study team norms, psychological safety, and experimentation culture as deliberately as I study roadmaps and user research, and I bring that same intentionality to every team I join.

I ask a lot of questions and analyze the data before I propose solutions, and I hold my assumptions loosely. My goal is to improve my customers' lives with every iteration rather than waiting for the perfect solution.

My career has taken me across two continents and a wide range of company sizes and structures, from Microsoft to internal startups where I wore every hat the product needed. Working across San Francisco, Prague, and distributed international teams has shaped how I communicate, how I build trust quickly in new environments, and how I think about the humans on the other side of the products I build.

I'm currently based in Prague and actively looking for a senior PM role focused on data or AI products. Remote and hybrid considered.
